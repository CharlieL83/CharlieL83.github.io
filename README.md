# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

## English

This app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on 
external servers. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your 
local device.

**iCloud Backup:** If you have enabled iCloud Backup on your device, 
your app data may be included in that backup as part of iOS's standard 
backup functionality. This process is entirely managed by Apple and 
subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
We have no access to this data at any time.

**Third Parties:** We do not share any data with third parties.

**Contact:** If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, 
please contact us at: [karellang@hotmail.com]
